OneDoc Patient App Terms of Service

Last updated: July 18, 2025

Service Description

OneDoc is a healthcare platform connecting patients with verified healthcare providers in South Africa. The service facilitates appointment booking, real-time tracking, and secure health record management.

User Responsibilities

  • Provide accurate health information for consultations
  • Respect healthcare provider time and appointments
  • Use the platform for legitimate healthcare purposes only
  • Maintain confidentiality of login credentials

Payment Terms

Payments are processed securely through Ozow. Consultation fees are clearly displayed before booking confirmation. Refunds are subject to cancellation policy terms.
